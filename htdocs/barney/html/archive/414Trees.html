Fall fun!

It's fall!! To celebrate, try to catch Barney & Friends episode #414-"Treemendous Trees." Colorful falling leaves are so visible to young children. It's a symbol easily associated with the changing season. This episode of Barney & Friends not only shows children what happens to trees in the fall, but it explains all about trees!

Show Synopsis:
Curtis is embarrassed to be a tree in the school play, but Barney assures him that it's a terrific role. Barney and the kids learn that trees need water and sunlight, and they remain healthy even when their leaves change color and drop off in the fall. The Adventure Screen shows many different types of trees in different environments. The kids are surprised to discover how many of their favorite foods and products come from trees. They learn about a great lumberjack named Paul Bunyan. Scooter and Miss Etta remind them that trees are also the home to many animals. Barney explains the importance of recycling. After learning so much about trees, Curtis is very excited to be a tree in the school play!
