Event Fact Sheet - Miami MetroZoo

Get Ready For ZOO-PER-DEE-DUPER Time!

Barney™ The Dinosaur will appear live at the Miami MetroZoo on September 29 & 30 - Barney's first ever appearance at the Miami MetroZoo.

WHO:

Everybody's favorite purple dinosaur!

WHAT:

Barney will perform two 20-minute sing-alongs each day, including songs from his brand new video, Barney, Let's Go To The Zoo. (Available in stores August 28.) Performances are free with paid admission to the zoo. Bring your blanket and your sunscreen and get ready for a "zoo-per-dee-duper" time! A special note to parents: there's no reserved seating, so be sure to come early!

WHEN:

Saturday, September 29 and Sunday September 30 at 11:00 a.m each day.

WHERE:

The Concert Field, Miami MetroZoo, 12400 SW 152nd St.
MiamiPublic Information Number: 305-251-0400

WHY:

Star of the top-rated Barney & Friends® PBS TV show (airing Sunday-Friday on WLRN/Miami), the Big Purple Dino is on the road to celebrate his new video, Barney, Let's Go To The Zoo. Filled with lots of fun, facts and surprises about our furry, feathered animal friends, it's the first Barney video ever shot entirely on location. Also this fall, look for a new line of cuddly zoo-themed plush toys, and interactive books from Scholastic for young learners.

SPONSORS:

Barney's Zoo Tour in Miami is sponsored by HIT Entertainment, WLRN/Miami, and Miami MetroZoo.

What better way to celebrate Barney's day at the zoo -- than to bring the fun to the children of Miami!
