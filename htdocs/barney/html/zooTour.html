The New Barney Zoo Tour
Is Dino-Mite!

Barney visits with koalas

Barney was a "zoo-per-dee-duper" success when thousands of families came out to see him live at the Main Meadow Zoo in Toronto! Barney fans of all ages joined their purple pal for a "zoo-pendous" sing-along at the zoo on August 25-26. Star of the top-rated TV show, Barney & Friends®, the purple sensation can be seen Monday - Friday on PBS station WNED in Buffalo, as well as your local PBS station.

The big, purple dino is on the road to celebrate his new video, Barney, Let's Go To The Zoo. Barney will perform 20-minute sing-alongs, including songs from the new video, when he takes his zoo tour to the next stop - the Cincinnati Zoo on September 8-9. See the schedule below for dates and locations of other zoos included on the tour. Click on the zoo name for further information.

Important Note: Due to the tragedies that occured on Sept. 11, we are postponing Barney's Zoo Tour appearance in Philadelphia. Barney's appearance will be rescheduled for Oct. 13-14. Our thoughts and prayers are with the victims and their families.
