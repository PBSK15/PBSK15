Character Descriptions

Barney
Fun-loving, child-like, highly energetic and incredibly friendly, Barney is the best friend a child could ever have (who just happens to be a 6-foot purple Tyrannosaurus Rex!)

Barney comes to life through the power of a child's imagination. In turn, Barney opens children's eyes to the joys of learning and discovery, to the wonders of make-believe, and to the beauty of unconditional love.

Baby Bop
Sweet, curious and innocent, Baby Bop is one of Barney's very best friends, and BJ's younger sister. Baby Bop is a three-year-old Triceratops with a lively imagination. Any game of pretend will find Baby Bop right in the middle, as a prima ballerina, a princess or an astronaut on a rocket ship.

BJ
BJ is an active seven-year-old Protoseratops who likes to run, jump and ride his scooter. Although he loves baseball, basketball and soccer, it's safe to say that BJ has never met a sport he didn't like! He enjoys playing with his friends and helping his younger sister, Baby Bop; together they provide opportunities to represent sibling relationships for our young viewing audience.
